# Day 3 - Role Based Prompting

## Objective

To understand how assigning different roles changes AI responses.

## Question Used

How can I build an AI startup?

## Prompt 1 (No Role)

How can I build an AI startup?

### Observation

The response was general and covered multiple areas briefly.

## Prompt 2 (Founder Role)

Act as a successful startup founder.

How can I build an AI startup?

### Observation

The response focused on business strategy, customer needs, funding, and growth.

## Prompt 3 (Developer Role)

Act as a senior AI developer.

How can I build an AI startup?

### Observation

The response focused on technology stack, AI models, APIs, deployment, and development process.

## Key Learnings

* AI responses change based on assigned roles.
* Different personas provide different perspectives.
* Role-based prompting improves relevance and quality.
