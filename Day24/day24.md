# Day 24 – Startup Business Strategy Report

## Overview

This repository contains my **Day 24 submission** for the Gen AI Bootcamp. The objective of this task was to transform the startup idea from Day 23 into a sustainable business strategy by evaluating its business model, revenue potential, customer acquisition plan, competitive advantage, and investment readiness.

---

## Startup Idea

**AI Career Platform for Final-Year Students**

An AI-powered career platform that helps students improve their chances of getting shortlisted by providing:

* Resume parsing and analysis
* Job Description (JD) matching
* ATS-friendly resume generation
* Skill gap analysis
* Personalized career recommendations

The platform aims to reduce the time students spend searching for jobs while improving resume quality and interview opportunities.

## Key Learnings

* A successful startup requires strong customer validation before scaling.
* Revenue and distribution are more important than adding extra features.
* Business Model Canvas helps identify strengths and weaknesses early.
* College partnerships provide a scalable B2B2C growth opportunity.
* Continuous customer feedback is essential for achieving Product-Market Fit.

## Final Verdict

**🟡 Validate**

The startup addresses a real problem faced by final-year students. While the MVP demonstrates strong technical potential, further validation through real users and college pilot programs is required before scaling. A successful validation phase can establish a sustainable and scalable business.
