## 💡 Key Learnings

### 1. Prompt Engineering for Long-Form Reports
Providing a well-structured master prompt with clear section headings guides Claude to produce organized, comprehensive output rather than fragmented answers.

### 2. Context Quality = Output Quality
The more accurate and detailed the personal information provided (especially birth time accuracy), the more specific and useful Claude's analysis becomes.

### 3. Managing AI Session Limits
Long reports may hit usage limits. Best practice is to:
- Save output progressively
- Note exactly where the report stopped
- Resume with: *"Please continue the report from [section name]"*

### 4. Critical Evaluation of AI-Generated Insights
AI-generated astrology content should be treated as a **reflective tool**, not a literal prediction. The value lies in:
- Prompting self-reflection
- Identifying patterns you may already sense
- Offering alternative perspectives on challenges

### 5. Claude's Strengths in Structured Analysis
Claude excels at synthesizing multi-domain inputs (astrology, psychology, career, relationships) into a cohesive narrative when given a clear framework.
