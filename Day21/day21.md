# Day 21 - Digital Footprint & Privacy Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective

Build an interactive cybersecurity-style dashboard that analyzes a user's reported digital footprint and estimates privacy exposure, ecosystem concentration, tracking surface, and potential data collection patterns.

---

## Focus Area

**Privacy Analytics & Cybersecurity Visualization**

The goal was to transform a list of commonly used applications into a visual privacy intelligence report while clearly separating facts from estimates.

---

## Technologies Used

* HTML5
* CSS3
* JavaScript
* SVG Visualizations
* Data Analytics
* Privacy Intelligence Concepts

## Dashboard Components

### Digital Footprint Score

Measures overall online platform exposure.

### Privacy Score

Estimates privacy posture based on ecosystem concentration and service usage.

### Exposure Heatmap

Visualizes estimated exposure levels across categories.

### Company Exposure Ranking

Ranks parent companies based on service usage.

### Data Collection Matrix

Displays potential categories of collected information.

### Risk Radar

Visual representation of estimated privacy risk areas.

### Digital Twin Profile

Provides estimated behavioural and technology usage patterns.

### Most Valuable Data Assets

Highlights the categories of information potentially most valuable to advertisers and platforms.

### Privacy Improvement Plan

Actionable recommendations for reducing digital exposure.

---

## Metrics Generated

* Digital Footprint Score
* Privacy Score
* Total Services Used
* Parent Company Count
* Ecosystem Concentration Score
* Estimated Tracking Surface

---

## Key Learnings

* Many digital services belong to a small number of parent companies.
* Ecosystem concentration increases potential data aggregation.
* Different apps contribute to different types of exposure.
* Privacy analysis requires distinguishing facts from estimates.
* Visualization helps users better understand digital risk.

---

## Challenges Faced

* Designing privacy metrics from limited inputs
* Clearly separating factual information from inferred estimates
* Presenting risk information responsibly
* Building an intuitive dashboard experience

---

## Outcome

Successfully created a premium-style privacy intelligence dashboard that visualizes digital footprint exposure and provides practical privacy improvement recommendations.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrated how data visualization, cybersecurity concepts, and privacy awareness can be combined to create engaging and educational analytics experiences.
